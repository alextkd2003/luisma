Air condition services

We have the necessary experience to solve the problems present in your home or office. If you need any service, do not hesitate to call us. Call us today to make an appointment.

Among the services we offer are:

Maintenance and Repair

It is done if your air conditioner is not working 100% or it has been more than 6 years since its last revision.

Among the problems you may encounter are:

  • Feel that it cools less.
  • There is greater energy consumption.
  • The AC is not clean or has a bad smell.
  • Feel the environment with high humidity.
  • It has been more than 6 months since the last revision.

* If you are in any of the above cases, it is likely that your CA has to be reviewed. Call us or contact us to review the problem and help you.

Among the maintenance services we offer is:

  • Duct cleaning.
  • Cleaning of the coil.
  • Cleaning of the outdoor unit.
  • Change of indoor or outdoor unit.

* Maintenance may involve the installation of parts of your equipment. If you want more details visit the installation service.
